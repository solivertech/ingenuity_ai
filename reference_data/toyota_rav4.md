> **Purpose:** This document provides authoritative reference data for evaluating used Carvana listings
> for the Toyota RAV4 across the 2021–2025 model years. Use this data to assess whether a given listing
> represents good value, identify the trim level, flag concerns, and contextualize pricing and features
> against known benchmarks.
>
> **Buyer profile:** Located in Phoenix, AZ. Budget up to $30,000. Interested in hybrid trims.
> Financing with approximately $3,000 down at ~7.5% APR over 60 months.

---

## Part 1 — Model Overview & Reliability

- **Generation in range:** 5th gen throughout 2021–2025. No major redesign in this window — all years are strong buys.
- **Reliability:** Excellent. Best long-term reliability and resale value in this group. Industry benchmark. Hybrid system is Toyota's 5th-generation — the most proven in the class.
- **Engines:** 2.5L 4-cylinder (203 hp, gas) | 2.5L + dual electric motors (219 hp, hybrid). Hybrid AWD is driven by a rear electric motor — not a traditional mechanical AWD system.
- **Gas MPG (FWD):** 27 city / 35 highway | AWD: 25–27 city / 32–35 highway depending on trim
- **Hybrid MPG (AWD):** 41 city / 38 highway (all hybrid trims include AWD standard)
- **Cargo (behind rear seats):** 37.6 cu ft (smallest in the compact SUV segment)
- **Rear legroom:** 37.8 inches (shortest in the compact SUV segment)
- **AWD:** Optional on gas trims (+$1,400). Standard on all hybrid trims — AWD via rear electric motor.
- **Towing:** 1,500 lbs (gas) | 1,750 lbs (hybrid)
- **Best value trim (used):** XLE (gas) | Hybrid XLE
- **Primary strength:** Best long-term reliability. Best resale value. Proven hybrid system. Hybrid XLE is widely considered the best all-around used compact SUV buy in this segment.
- **Notable weakness:** Smallest cargo space and rear legroom in this group. Rear seat can feel cramped for taller passengers. Interior feels dated vs. 2023+ CR-V and Sportage. High used demand keeps prices elevated.

## Part 5 — Evaluation Flags & Red Flags

### Always flag

- **RAV4 "Prime" listings:** This is a PHEV — higher value, different use case. Note it is not a standard hybrid and will be priced higher.
- **Mileage vs. age:** Flag if mileage is unusually high for the year. Rule of thumb: more than 15,000 miles per year is above average. More than 20,000 miles per year is high and warrants noting.
- **Shipping cost:** If shipping is listed, add to total price for true cost comparison. Flag when shipping is $0 vs. when it adds cost.

### Positive signals

- RAV4 Hybrid at or below the pricing range table — these hold value well and sell fast
- Any hybrid with $0 shipping to Phoenix
- Any listing where Carvana's quoted monthly payment is at or below the estimated payment at 7.5% APR / 60 months / $3,000 down

### Concern signals

- Price more than 10% above the used market range for that year/trim
- Mileage above 70,000
- Any listing without a VIN (data quality issue — cannot verify history)

## Part 6 — Analyst Notes for Output Formatting

When producing analysis based on this reference document and live Carvana listing data:

1. **Always note the AWD status** — whether it is standard (all hybrids), optional (+$1,400 on gas trims), or unavailable
2. **Always note hybrid vs. gas** — and if hybrid, confirm it is the Toyota 5th-gen system (not Prime/PHEV)
3. **Compare estimated monthly payment** against the buyer's financing profile ($3,000 down, 7.5% APR, 60 months) for each listing
4. **Do not treat missing shipping cost as $0** — label it "shipping unavailable" and note it may add to total cost
5. **Rank by value score first**, but provide qualitative commentary that goes beyond the score — a high-scoring listing with a concerning mileage-to-age ratio should have that noted explicitly
6. **Keep the final recommendation concrete** — name a specific listing (year, make, model, trim, price) as the top pick with a one-sentence rationale
7. **Note that RAV4 Hybrid prices are elevated by high demand** — a listing at the top of the range is not necessarily a bad deal, but one at the low end is a strong positive signal
