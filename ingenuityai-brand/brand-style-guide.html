Logo usage — do and don't

Do

Use on dark teal or white backgrounds only

Maintain clear space equal to mark height on all sides

Use the contained icon for app icons and favicons

Keep "AI" in teal — always contrasting with "Ingenuity"

Scale proportionally — never stretch or distort

Minimum wordmark size: 120px wide

Don't

Recolor the mark or wordmark outside the brand palette

Place on busy or photographic backgrounds

Add drop shadows, outlines, or glow effects

Use the wordmark below 120px wide

Rearrange elements or alter letter spacing

Use grayscale versions of the mark

Brand voice

Precise, not jargon-heavy

Say "finds the data you're looking for" — not "leverages AI-driven schema extraction pipelines."

Confident, not boastful

Let the capability speak. Avoid superlatives like "the most powerful" or "revolutionary."

Human, not robotic

Write like you're explaining it to a smart friend, not drafting a press release.

Active voice always

"IngenuityAI monitors the page and sends you an alert" — not "alerts are sent when conditions are detected."
